Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Business Information

When you create a booking system, we collect:

  • Business name and contact details
  • Service offerings and pricing
  • Business hours and availability
  • Address and location information
  • Logo and branding preferences

Customer Information

When customers book appointments, we collect:

  • Name and contact information
  • Service preferences and booking details
  • Payment information (if applicable)
  • Communication preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

For Businesses

We use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your booking system
  • Process customer bookings and payments
  • Provide analytics and reporting
  • Send important notifications and updates
  • Improve our services and user experience

For Customers

We use your information to:

  • Process and confirm bookings
  • Send booking confirmations and reminders
  • Communicate with businesses about appointments
  • Provide customer support

Financial Data Processing

We process financial information for:

  • Processing customer payments securely through Stripe
  • Calculating and distributing business earnings
  • Generating financial reports and statements
  • Managing payout processing and tracking
  • Compliance with financial regulations and tax requirements

3. Data Protection and Security

We take data protection seriously and implement appropriate security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your data
  • Regular Backups: Data is backed up regularly to prevent loss
  • Secure Infrastructure: We use secure, compliant hosting providers

GDPR Compliance

We comply with GDPR and other applicable data protection laws. Your rights include:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Transfer your data to another service
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data

4. Data Sharing

We may share your information in limited circumstances:

  • With Businesses: Share booking details with the business you're booking with
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (payment processors, email services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze website usage and improve our services
  • Provide personalized experiences

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Access: View and update your personal information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Complaint: Report privacy concerns to data protection authorities

7. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@showupmedia.org
  • Website: showupmedia.org

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Emailing the address on file
  • Posting on our website
  • Updating the "Last updated" date below
