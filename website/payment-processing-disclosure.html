2. Payment Processing Flow

?? Settlement-Based Payout Guarantee

We guarantee that businesses receive their earnings after Stripe settlement clears, ensuring all funds are available before transfers are made.

How It Works:

Step 1: Customer Books Service

Customer selects service and pays online via Stripe

Step 2: Payment Processed

Payment goes to Show Up Media's Stripe account for security and compliance

Step 3: Stripe Settlement (2-7 Business Days)

Stripe transfers funds to Show Up Media's bank account (standard UK processing time)

Step 4: Business Payout (Next Business Day)

Show Up Media manually transfers settled earnings to business bank account

5. Legal & Compliance

Financial Regulations Compliance

  • PCI-DSS Compliance: All payment processing is PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant via Stripe
  • Anti-Money Laundering (AML): We comply with UK AML regulations
  • Know Your Customer (KYC): Business verification required for payouts
  • Financial Conduct Authority (FCA): We operate as a payment facilitator under FCA guidelines
  • GDPR Compliance: All financial data processed in compliance with GDPR

Business Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate business information for verification
  • Maintain valid bank account or payment processor details
  • Comply with UK tax obligations on earnings
  • Report any suspicious payment activity immediately

Refund Policy
