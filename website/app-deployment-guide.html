📋 Deployment Instructions

🎯 Quick Start for Pro/Premium Users

Your booking system is already configured for mobile app deployment. Follow these steps to get your app live:

Step 1: Configure Your App

Access your admin dashboard and navigate to Settings → Mobile App Configuration.

  • Set your app name and branding
  • Configure push notification settings
  • Set up payment gateways (Pro/Premium)
  • Customize app colors and logo
Step 2: Download App Builder

Download the Show Up Media App Builder for your platform:

📥 Download App Builder
Step 3: Build Your App

Launch the App Builder and follow the wizard:

  • Select your business template
  • Choose app features (Pro/Premium)
  • Configure payment providers
  • Set up push notifications
  • Customize branding and colors
Step 4: Test & Deploy

Test your app on device and deploy to app stores:

  • Testing: Use built-in simulator or connect physical device
  • App Store: Submit to Apple App Store (requires Apple Developer account)
  • Google Play: Submit to Google Play Store (requires Google Play Developer account)
  • Web App: Deploy to your custom domain (included with Premium)
Step 5: Launch & Promote

Once live, promote your app to customers:

  • Add app download links to your website
  • Create QR codes for easy access
  • Set up app store optimization
  • Enable customer reviews and ratings
  • Monitor analytics and performance

🔧 Technical Requirements

For iOS Apps:

  • Development: Xcode 14+, iOS 14+ target
  • Apple Developer Account: $99/year
  • App Store Connect: For distribution
  • Push Notifications: Apple Push Notification Service
  • Payment: Apple Pay integration

For Android Apps:

  • Development: Android Studio, Java/Kotlin
  • Google Play Developer Account: $25 one-time fee
  • Push Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Payment: Google Pay integration

For Web Apps:

  • Development: Progressive Web App standards
  • Hosting: Included with Premium plan
  • Domain: Custom domain with Premium plan
  • HTTPS: SSL certificate included
