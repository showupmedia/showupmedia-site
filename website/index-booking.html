Common Questions

How does the self-build booking system work?
+
Choose from 5 professional templates, upload your logo, pick your brand colors, add your services and pricing, then launch! The whole process takes about 5 minutes and you'll have a fully functional booking system ready to share with customers.
What's included in the £6.99/month plan?
+
Everything you need - your chosen template, automated email reminders, online payment collection, customer dashboard, calendar sync, and ongoing support. No hidden fees or setup costs.
Can I customize the templates?
+
Yes! Each template is fully customizable with your logo, brand colors, services, pricing, and business information. You can make it match your exact brand identity.

No technical skills required! The builder is designed to be user-friendly with simple step-by-step instructions. If you can use social media, you can build your booking system.

+
Immediately! Once you complete the 5-minute setup process, your booking system goes live instantly. You can start sharing your booking link with customers right away.
