FAQ

Common Questions

What do I need to provide?
+
Just share access to your stream VODs, YouTube videos or any long-form footage. We'll handle everything from there — finding the best moments, editing, formatting and delivering the finished clips.
What's the difference between Polished and Raw Cuts?
+
Polished clips are fully produced — sound effects, dynamic subtitles, on-screen text and tightly edited for maximum retention. They're ready to post straight away. Raw cuts are simply chopped and platform-formatted clips with no production work — great if you want to add your own style on top, or just need the volume fast.
How long does delivery take?
+
Turnaround depends on the volume ordered. Typically 3–5 business days for a 30-clip order, 5–7 days for 60 clips, and 7–10 days for 90 clips. We'll confirm an exact timeline after you order.
Which platforms are the clips optimised for?
+
TikTok, Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts by default. All clips are formatted in 9:16 vertical, with platform-appropriate captions and timing. Let us know if you need a specific platform priority.
Do I own the clips?
+
Yes, completely. All delivered clips come with full commercial rights. Post them anywhere, monetise them, repurpose them however you like — forever. One-time payment covers all future usage.
Is payment secure?
+
Yes. All payments are processed through Stripe — bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption on every transaction. Your card details are never stored on our servers.
